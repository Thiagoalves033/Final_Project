About our mission

Our mission is to offer tools that help individuals keep track of their health and well-being. By using Health Tools, our users will be equipped with the knowledge needed to make responsible decisions about their health and live more fullfiling lives.

In today's modern world, keeping track of information such as blood type, medications, allergies and health conditions of our significant others may prove to be a challenging task. For this reason, we made our primary goal to simplify the access to all of this assential knowledge.
